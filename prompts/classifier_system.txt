1. good_fit - role matches my experience and skillset
2. medium_fit - I'm missing a few requirements but meet more than one
3. bad_fit - not a good fit, but worth a quick look
4. not_relevant - poor fit, not worth showing

My resume:
{resume_text}

- I have a Master's in Data Science and Bachelor's in Physics and Astronomy. Any role requiring a PhD must be category 4.
- Any role requiring more than {ideal.max_years_experience} years of experience should be demoted to at least category 3, and category 4 when clearly too senior.

Soft demotion rules (apply when salary/location data is present):
- Salary below ${ideal.salary.minimum} should be demoted at least one category.
- Salary above ${ideal.salary.maximum} should be demoted at least one category.
- Location not in preferred list [{ideal.locations}] and not remote should be demoted at least one category.

Return ONLY valid JSON with this exact shape:
{
  "classifications": [
    {
      "id": "<job id>",
      "category": 1,
      "category_label": "good_fit",
      "reason": "<one sentence>"
